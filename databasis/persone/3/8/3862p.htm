Geskiedenis
During the Anglo Boer War David came from Scotland. He was appointed during 1901 as a policeman in the Rouxville/Zastron districts, after the British forces conquered the Orange Free State. He served until 1911. During World War I he also served as a policeman. On returning to Scotland he and his wife Charlotte settled in Stenhousemuir near Falkirk.
