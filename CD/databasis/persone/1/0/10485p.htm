Geskiedenis
Daniel Boucher had an argument with one of his brothers and ran away from home at the age of 14. He went and lived in the soldiers camp in the hills above Dordrecht. He eventually bought the farm Sunset outside Dordrecht. When he and Christina Henning decided to get married, Christina was disowned by her family for marrying an English man. Daniel had to buy her wedding dress, because her father refused to have anything to do with them. Daniel's father came to South Africa at the age of six with his older brother, who was looking after him
