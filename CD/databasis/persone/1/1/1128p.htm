Geskiedenis
Susan attended Westville Junior and Senior Primary school and matriculated in 1990 at Westville Girls high school.

She worked as a Board certified Surgical Technologist at various hospitals in Louisville, Kentucky, USA and surrounding areas and is currently working at Norton Audubon Hospital In Occupational Therapy rehabilitation department in intensive care unit.

As a hobby she do gym group exercise classes.

She and her children live in Louisville, Kentucky, USA
