Geskiedenis
Janice stems from the German speaking farming community at Wartburg, Natal. During 2005 her employer, Pricewaterhouse Coopers seconded her to a branch in Charlotte, North Carolina, USA where she met Adrian Henning, who was also a South African and transferred in 2007 to the Charlotte branch. During March 2021 Janice was appointed as Audit Director at Coca Cola Consolidated, a public company with headquarters in Charlotte NC
