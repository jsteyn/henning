Geskiedenis
Paulina and her husband were married in the Catholic church, Pietersburg. Approximately 1928- 29 they emigrated to Australia. Their first two children, Gordon Henning Mitchell and Hector Taylor Mttchell were left in an orphanage in Johannesburg when they emigrated. Only their two younger sons Roy Kerr Breamer Mitchell and Kenneth Ross Mitchell accompanied them. In Australia they had four more children: Mary (Maria) Headrick Mitchell, Douglas Mitchell, Paulina Elizabeth Mitchell and Eric Mitchell
