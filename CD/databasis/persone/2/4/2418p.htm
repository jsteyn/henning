Geskiedenis
Robert grew up in Edenvale, Johannesburg and attended Rembrandt Park Primary School since his Mom was a music teacher there. He enjoyed playing tennis at the Lombardy East Tennis Club. From there he went to High School at Bishop Bavin St Georges in Bedfordview as his dad was teaching there and it was at this time that his dad joined the Anglican Priesthood.
They then moved to the Rectory of St Margaret's Anglican Church in Kempton Park. Robert studied computing and got his MCSE (Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer) and worked for a year in industry but returned to WITS University to study teaching.
After qualifying as a teacher in 2004 he moved to Cape Town to be a boarding house master and English Teacher at Wynberg Boys' High School. In Cape Town he met his future wife, Kristin Lorenc, who is now a medical doctor (radiologist).
During October 2020 they relocated to Rugby in the United Kingdom where Kristin is working for the NHS at University Hospital of Coventry and Warwickshire and Robert is teaching.
