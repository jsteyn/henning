Hierdie laserskyf bevat inligting ten opsigte van die geskiedenis en geslagsregisters van al die Henning families in Suid-Afrika, wat voor 1850 by die Kaap van Goeie Hoop aangekom het. Dit kan dus as die FAMILIEKRONIEK VAN DIE HENNING FAMILIES van Suid-Afrika beskou word. Welcome to the world of the Henning families of South Africa. This compact disc contains information on the history and genealogies of all the Henning families in South Africa who arrived at the Cape of Good Hope before 1850. It can therefore be regarded as the FAMILY CRONICLE OF THE HENNING FAMILIES of South Africa.
